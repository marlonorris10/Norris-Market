Rooted in Community

Something new is growing at Darrell Norris Greenhouse.

Built on the longstanding tradition of Darrell Norris Greenhouse, Norris Market was created to support small businesses, showcase unique local goods, and give families a welcoming place to gather throughout the seasons.

From seasonal markets to special events, Norris Market is designed to celebrate community, connection, and local businesses.

A curated fall market for families, kids, and the community.

The Norris Harvest Festival will include a market setup inside Darrell Norris Greenhouse retail for the public, along with fall festival-style activities for kids and families.

Activities are still being finalized, but the goal is to create a family-friendly fall destination filled with shopping, food, seasonal fun, and community connection.

Helpful Information

Vendor FAQ

Can I apply for one day only?

Yes. Vendors may apply for one day, one weekend, or all four event days.

Do vendors have to be approved?

Yes. Vendors will be reviewed and approved to help maintain quality, variety, and a balanced mix of product categories.

Do I need to bring my own tent?

Yes. Vendors are responsible for providing their own tent, tables, chairs, and display materials.

Is electric or water available?

Yes. Electric and water access are available for vendors. Please include those needs on your application.
